Affordable Housing. Institutional Discipline.

RAFA Homes is a Malaysian-based housing developer focused on large-scale, affordable housing developments structured to meet institutional, cooperative and financial partner requirements.

About RAFA Homes

RAFA Homes (M) Sdn. Bhd. is principally involved in the development and delivery of affordable and community-based housing projects across Malaysia. The company specialises in structuring projects that balance affordability, execution discipline and financing sustainability.

Our developments are undertaken through phased implementation, consortium-based delivery and structured financing coordination with cooperatives and financial institutions.

RAFA KSE Development functions as the Group’s project execution and technical support arm, providing development management and delivery capability in support of RAFA Homes’ projects.

Why Partner with RAFA Homes

Total Financing Solutions

RAFA Homes provides end-to-end financing solutions covering project structuring, funding coordination, participant onboarding and end-financing alignment in collaboration with cooperatives and financial institutions.

Consortium-Based Delivery Capability

Project execution is supported by a consortium of experienced contractors, project managers and strategic partners, enabling phased delivery of multi-thousand unit developments.

Integrated Risk Management

Risks are managed holistically across land readiness, financing, construction, delivery and governance through structured agreements and oversight.

“RAFA Homes is structured to deliver housing developments that are financially disciplined, institutionally aligned and execution-ready, with clear cash-flow visibility and phased implementation.”

— Managing Director & Executive Director, RAFA Homes

Management & Governance

Managing Director

Provides overall corporate leadership and strategic direction, with primary responsibility for institutional engagement, partnerships and business development.

Executive Director

Oversees strategy execution, project structuring and business development, translating development concepts into bankable and executable projects.

Project Director

Responsible for end-to-end project execution including construction coordination, delivery schedules and quality control across all phases.

Finance Director

Leads financial structuring, funding coordination, cash flow management and engagement with banks and cooperatives.


Board of Advisory

The Management Team is supported by a Board of Advisory comprising experienced professionals in finance, development and governance, providing independent guidance and institutional assurance.

Projects & Funding Framework

  • Scale: Multi-phase developments involving several thousand housing units.
  • Execution: Phased construction aligned with funding drawdowns.
  • Financing: Coordinated structures involving cooperatives, financial institutions and strategic partners.
  • Risk Allocation: Clearly defined responsibilities across land, construction, financing and delivery.
  • Governance: Management controls supported by Board of Advisory oversight.

Delivering Affordable Homes, Building Sustainable Futures

RAFA Homes is a Malaysian property development company dedicated to delivering affordable, scalable and community-focused housing solutions through structured financing and smart partnerships.

About RAFA Homes

RAFA Homes focuses on the development and execution of affordable housing projects across Malaysia, with strong emphasis on FELDA New Generation housing, cooperative-led developments and large-scale community schemes.

The company structures cost-efficient developments supported by strategic financing, disciplined project management and long-term sustainability for beneficiaries.

RAFA KSE Development operates as the Group’s project execution and development support arm, providing technical, structuring and delivery capabilities for RAFA Homes’ developments.

Our Core Focus

Affordable Housing

Delivery of cost-based and affordable housing models tailored for Malaysian families and second-generation communities.

Large-Scale Development

Execution of multi-phase housing developments involving thousands of units through structured rollouts.

Smart Partnerships

Management Team

Managing Director

Leads the overall business direction of RAFA Homes, with primary focus on corporate leadership, strategic partnerships and business development. Responsible for aligning projects with institutional, cooperative and government stakeholders.

Executive Director

Oversees strategy execution, project structuring and business development. Plays a key role in financial alignment, partner negotiations and translating development concepts into bankable projects.

Project Director

Responsible for end-to-end project execution, including construction coordination, timeline management, quality control and delivery across all development phases.

Finance Director

Leads financial planning, funding structures, cash flow management and coordination with banks, cooperatives and funding partners to ensure financial discipline and sustainability.


Board of Advisory

The Management Team is supported by a Board of Advisory comprising experienced professionals in finance, development, governance and strategic advisory, providing independent guidance and oversight to strengthen decision-making and institutional confidence.

Projects & Funding Framework

RAFA Homes undertakes large-scale affordable housing developments through a structured and phased framework designed to meet institutional financing requirements and long-term sustainability.

  • Development Scale: Multi-phase housing developments ranging from several thousand units per project.
  • Phased Execution: Progressive construction and delivery to align with funding drawdowns and cash flow discipline.
  • Financing Structure: Coordinated funding involving cooperatives, financial institutions and strategic partners with clear role delineation.
  • Risk Allocation: Defined responsibilities across land readiness, construction, financing and delivery.
  • Governance: Oversight supported by management controls and Board of Advisory guidance.

This framework ensures transparency, manageability and institutional confidence throughout the project lifecycle.
